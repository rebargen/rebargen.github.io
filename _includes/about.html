About

We are a specialized provider of rebar detailing services, delivering accurate and reliable solutions to support construction projects across international markets. Our core expertise includes the preparation of detailed shop drawings and bar bending schedules (BBS), developed in accordance with project specifications, applicable codes, and industry standards.

With a strong focus on precision, quality, and consistency, we assist contractors, consultants, and fabricators in streamlining their construction processes. Our detailing outputs are carefully coordinated to minimize errors, reduce material wastage, and ensure smooth execution at site.

Driven by professionalism and technical expertise, our team is committed to delivering timely and cost-effective services without compromising on accuracy. We aim to build long-term partnerships with our clients by consistently providing dependable results and contributing to the successful completion of their projects.
